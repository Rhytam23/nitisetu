# 🎙️ Niti-Setu: Final Demo Presentation Script

**Project Goal:** Bridging the gap between complex government policy and citizen eligibility via AI.
**Key Feature:** Frictionless access, Voice Intelligence, and RAG-powered Policy Verification.

---

## 🛠️ Demo Execution Guide: "What We Have To Do"

Follow these exact steps during the presentation to sync with the script:

1.  **START:** Open the **Landing Page**. Ensure the smooth animations are visible.
2.  **ACTION 1:** Scroll down slightly to show "Built for Resilience" features, then scroll back up.
3.  **ACTION 2:** Click the **"Start Free Consultation"** button. (Point out there is NO login barrier—direct access).
4.  **ACTION 3:** On the **Farmer Profile** page, first use the **Language Selector** (top right) to show multi-lingual support.
5.  **ACTION 4:** Click the **"Voice Input"** button. Speak a sample sentence: *"I am a farmer from Punjab with 3 acres of wheat land."* 
    *   *Note: If voice fails or environment is noisy, manually type into the fields.*
6.  **ACTION 5:** Ensure **PM-KISAN** or **PM-KUSUM** is selected. Click **"Check Eligibility Now"**.
7.  **ACTION 6:** Wait for the **"Consulting AI Units..."** loader to finish (shows the RAG process).
8.  **ACTION 7:** Scroll to the **Result Card**. Point out the **"AI Verdict"**, the **"Proof Quote"** (extracted from real PDFs), and the **"Required Documents"** list.
9.  **FINISH:** Return to the top of the page using the **Back Arrow**.

## 🎤 Presentation Script (~3 Minutes)

### [0:00 - 0:45] The Problem: Niti-Setu" - Voice-Based Scheme Eligibility Engine
"Good morning. We are Team KrishiSolve. 

Every year, the Indian government launches brilliant schemes for our farmers. But there is a silent crisis: the **Awareness Gap**. 

Policy guidelines are buried in 50-page bureaucratic PDFs. For a farmer in rural India, these documents are a maze of legalese. They ask themselves: *'Am I eligible? What documents do I need?'* Usually, they have to pay a middleman just to get an answer. We decided to fix that."

### [0:45 - 1:30] The Solution: Direct, Frictionless AI
"Enter **Niti-Setu**. As you see on the screen, we’ve built a premium, high-performance gateway that removes all barriers. 

We’ve removed the login section entirely. No passwords, no sign-ups. Just instant clarity. 

Our engine isn't just a chatbot. It’s a **Real-time AI Consultant**. It uses **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** to parse official government orders in milliseconds. It doesn't guess; it verifies."

### [1:30 - 2:30] The Live Demo: Voice & Logic
"Let’s start a consultation. (Action: Click Get Started)

Notice the interface: clean, premium, and multi-lingual. For many farmers, typing is a barrier. That’s why we’ve integrated **Voice Intelligence**. 

(Action: Click Voice Input)
The farmer speaks his context—his location, his land size, his crops. Our engine maps these regional dialects into structured data.

(Action: Click Check Eligibility)
Now, watch. The system isn't just checking a database. It’s analyzing the latest **PDF Guidelines**. It’s cross-referencing landholding limits and category exclusions against the farmer's specific profile."

### [2:30 - 3:15] The Verdict: Absolute Transparency
"And here is our result: The **AI Proof Card**. 

We don't just say 'Yes' or 'No.' We provide the exact **verbatim quote** from the government policy that justifies the decision. We provide a tailored checklist of required documents—Aadhaar, Jamabandi, or Bank Passbooks. 

We provide the **Citations** for legal clarity. This is transparency at scale."

### [3:15 - 3:30] Closing
"Niti-Setu is more than a tool; it’s a bridge to trust. By translating complex legalese into instant, voice-activated clarity, we ensure that the benefits of progress reach the hands that feed the nation. 

Thank you."
