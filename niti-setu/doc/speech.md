# 🎙️ Niti-Setu: Project Presentation Script
**Role:** Lead Presenter (Student)
**Target:** Judging Panel
**Duration:** ~2 minutes 45 seconds (approx. 430 words)

---

## [0:00 - 0:30] Introduction & The "Awareness Gap"
"Good morning, esteemed judges. We are Team [Team Name], and today we’re excited to present **Niti-Setu**—an AI-powered bridge designed to simplify governance for every Indian citizen."

"In India, thousands of life-changing government schemes are launched every year. However, the 'Awareness Gap' remains massive. Why? Because these policies are often buried in 50-page bureaucratic PDFs that are impossible for a common citizen, especially a farmer, to decode. Citizens are left wondering: *'Am I eligible? How do I apply?'*"

---

## [0:30 - 1:00] The Solution: Niti-Setu
"That’s where Niti-Setu comes in. We’ve built a premium **Eligibility Engine** that acts as a real-time AI Consultant. Instead of reading through complex documents, a user simply talks to our platform."

"Under the hood, Niti-Setu uses an advanced AI reasoning engine. It doesn't just search for text; it parses the latest government guidelines via vector embeddings and RAG—Retrieval-Augmented Generation—to verify policy logic in real-time."

---

## [1:00 - 2:15] The Prototype Demo
"Let’s walk you through our live prototype. As you can see on the screen, we’ve prioritized a high-end, accessible UI. We start by selecting a preferred language—because accessibility begins with a language the user understands."

"Now, imagine a farmer checking his eligibility for an Agricultural scheme. He enters his basic profile—age, landholding, and income. As he hits 'Check Eligibility,' our **Consultant Engine** springs into action. It retrieves the specific PDF guideline for that scheme, verifies the user’s data against the legal logic, and identifies any 'Proof' required."

"And here is the 'AI Verdict.' Instead of a simple 'Yes' or 'No,' the user gets a **Proof Card**. It clearly lists which documents they need and provides a transparent explanation of the decision, all powered by our backend reasoning engine."

---

## [2:15 - 2:45] Impact & Vision
"The impact here is two-fold: Transparency and Scalability. By translating bureaucratic complexity into voice-activated clarity, we empower the most vulnerable sections of society."

"Our vision for Niti-Setu is to become the universal gateway for every Indian scheme, providing a seamless, AI-driven experience that ensures no citizen is left behind due to a lack of information. Thank you for your time, and we’re now ready for your questions."

**[End of Script]**
